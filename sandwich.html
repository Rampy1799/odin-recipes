Sandwich Recipe

Ready to eat tasty Sandwich? It's a quick recipe and nourishing breakfast.
All you need to have are bread, onion, tomato and mayonnaise to do this.

Ingredients needed:

  • mayonnaise
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Bread

Steps to make sandwiches:

  1. Chop those onions and tomatoes, if you have cucumbers and cheese slizes you can add them as well.
  2. Add Mayonnaise and this chopped veggies to a bowl.
  3. Add salt, chilli powder, Pepper to the bowl and mix it thoroughly.
  4. Add this mixture to the bread and fry them.
  5. That's it, sandwiches are ready. "Bon Appetit!"
